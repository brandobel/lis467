About



Hello world!

I’m a Library & Information Science graduate student at Simmons University in Boston, MA. I’m passionate about organization and systems, and that goes for just about anything, including my spice rack…


My goal is to build welcoming spaces, both online and in the “real world.” Spaces where people can easily find and retrieve the information they’re looking for. Spaces where stories are told, and stories are heard. Spaces where good design leads to good living. I want to answer the question, "What does hospitality look like in a digital age?"


I’m originally from the Mitten State, and I moved to Boston with my wife Katherine and our giant cat Stanley in December of 2017. I’m a bibliophile, an espresso aficionado, a wannabe foodie, and an all-around enthusiast. A few of my hobbies are cooking, traveling, playing piano, and competing in Wikipedia races.


I’m a fan of the Apple ecosystem, and some of my favorite apps for getting through the day are:

  • Things | task and project management
  • Spark | email, plus calendars on Mac
  • Bear | note taking
  • Brackets | coding
  • Instapaper | bookmarking and reading
  • Fantastical | calendars
